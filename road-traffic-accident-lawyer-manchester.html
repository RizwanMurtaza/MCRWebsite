What to Do After a Road Traffic Accident

üö® Immediate Steps After an Accident

  • Ensure safety: Move to safe location if possible
  • Call emergency services: 999 if injuries or road blocked
  • Exchange details: Name, address, insurance, registration
  • Take photos: Accident scene, vehicle damage, road conditions
  • Get witness details: Names and contact information
  • Note details: Time, weather, road conditions, what happened
  • Seek medical attention: Even if injuries seem minor
  • Contact solicitor: Get legal advice as soon as possible
