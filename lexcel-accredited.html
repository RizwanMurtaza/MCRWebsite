Lexcel Accredited

MCR Solicitors are delighted to announce we have been accredited with the Law Society award, Lexcel, in recognising excellence in standards.

What is Lexcel?

Lexcel is the Law Society's  quality standard for client care, compliance and practice management. Effectively a badge of excellence, the scheme certifies that participating law firms can demonstrate that certain standards have been met through independent assessment.

Lexcel is our legal practice quality mark for client care, compliance and practice management. It sets the standard for:

  • Client care
  • Risk management
  • People management
  • Structure and strategy
  • Financial management
  • Information management
  • File and case management

What does this mean for our valued clients?

It means that when you choose MCR Solicitors, you can trust that you are receiving legal assistance of the highest calibre. Our Lexcel Accreditation is a testament to our commitment to providing you with the highest levels of service, tailored to meet your individual needs and exceed your expectations.
