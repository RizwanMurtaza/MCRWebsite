What is No Time Limit (NTL) Status?

No Time Limit (NTL) is a form of permanent immigration status in the UK, historically granted to certain Commonwealth citizens. Our Manchester NTL immigration lawyers explain the key features:

  • Permanent residence - Right to remain in UK indefinitely
  • Work authorization - No restrictions on employment
  • Travel freedom - Can leave and return to UK freely
  • Family benefits - Can sponsor family members
  • Path to citizenship - Can apply for British citizenship
  • Limited new grants - Rarely granted under current rules
