What is a Non Molestation Order?

A Non Molestation Order is a court order designed to protect you from domestic violence, abuse, or harassment. Our Manchester domestic violence solicitors explain what it covers:

  • Physical violence prevention - Stops assault and battery
  • Harassment protection - Prevents unwanted contact or following
  • Threatening behaviour - Prohibits verbal threats and intimidation
  • Psychological abuse - Covers emotional and mental abuse
  • Indirect contact - Stops contact through third parties
  • Social media harassment - Includes online abuse and stalking

Types of Behaviour Covered

Our Manchester domestic violence specialists secure protection against various forms of abuse:

💥 Physical Violence

  • Assault and battery - Any unwanted physical contact
  • Domestic violence - Hitting, slapping, pushing, punching
  • Sexual violence - Unwanted sexual contact or assault
  • Physical intimidation - Threatening gestures or behaviour
  • Weapon threats - Threatening with objects or weapons

📞 Harassment and Stalking

  • Unwanted contact - Phone calls, texts, emails, letters
  • Following or stalking - Pursuing or watching
  • Workplace harassment - Attending work or contacting colleagues
  • Social media abuse - Online harassment and threats
  • Third-party contact - Using friends/family to make contact

🗣️ Verbal and Emotional Abuse

  • Threatening language - Verbal threats of violence
  • Abusive behaviour - Shouting, screaming, verbal attacks
  • Psychological abuse - Mental cruelty and control
  • Intimidating behaviour - Creating fear and anxiety
  • Coercive control - Controlling and manipulative behaviour
