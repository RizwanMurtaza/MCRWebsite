What is the Skilled Worker Visa?

The Skilled Worker Visa replaced the Tier 2 (General) work visa in December 2020. Our Manchester points-based immigration lawyers explain the key features:

  • Points-based system - Must score 70+ points to qualify
  • Sponsored employment - Need job offer from licensed UK sponsor
  • Skilled occupation - Job must be at RQF Level 3 or above
  • English language - Demonstrate English proficiency
  • Financial requirements - Maintenance funds or sponsor support
  • Settlement pathway - Apply for ILR after 5 years

Skilled Worker Visa Points System - Manchester Solicitors

Our Skilled Worker Visa solicitors in Manchester help you understand and meet the points requirements:

📊 Mandatory Points (50 points required)

  • Job offer from licensed sponsor - 20 points (essential)
  • Job at appropriate skill level - 20 points (RQF Level 3+)
  • English language proficiency - 10 points (B1 level minimum)

💰 Salary Points (20+ points required)

Choose ONE salary option to get required points:

  • £26,200+ per year - 20 points
  • Going rate for occupation - 20 points
  • £23,040-£26,199 - 0 points (but can get tradeable points)

🔄 Tradeable Points (Optional - if salary below £26,200)

  • PhD relevant to job - 10 points
  • PhD in STEM subject - 20 points
  • Job in shortage occupation - 20 points
  • New entrant - 20 points (under 26, PhD graduate, etc.)

Skilled Worker Visa Requirements - Manchester Immigration Lawyers

Our Manchester Skilled Worker Visa specialists help clients meet these essential requirements:

👔 Employment Requirements

  • Valid job offer - From Home Office licensed sponsor
  • Certificate of Sponsorship - Sponsor must assign CoS
  • Genuine job - Real position, not created for visa purposes
  • Appropriate skill level - RQF Level 3 or higher
  • Salary threshold - Meet minimum salary requirements

🎓 Qualification Requirements

  • Relevant qualifications - Education/experience for the role
  • Academic credentials - Degree certificates if required
  • Professional qualifications - Industry-specific accreditations
  • UK NARIC assessment - Foreign qualifications may need evaluation

💷 Financial Requirements

  • Maintenance funds - £1,270 for 28 days (if sponsor doesn't certify)
  • Immigration Health Surcharge - £624 per year of visa
  • Sponsor certification - May cover maintenance requirements
  • Dependants' funds - Additional funds for family members

Skilled Worker Visa Application Process

⚡ Time-Sensitive Process Warning

CoS expires after 3 months - must apply before deadline. Contact our Manchester work visa solicitors immediately after receiving job offer.

📅 Step-by-Step Application Process

Step 1: Secure job offer from licensed sponsor

Step 2: Employer assigns Certificate of Sponsorship (CoS)

Step 3: Calculate points and gather evidence

Step 4: Complete online application with our legal support

Step 5: Attend biometric appointment

Step 6: Await decision (usually 3-8 weeks)

Step 7: Receive visa and plan UK relocation
