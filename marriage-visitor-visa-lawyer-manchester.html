What is a Marriage Visitor Visa?

A Marriage Visitor Visa allows someone from outside the UK to come to the UK specifically to get married or enter into a civil partnership. Our Manchester wedding visa solicitors explain the key features:

  • 6-month validity - Stay in UK up to 6 months maximum
  • Marriage purpose only - Cannot work, study, or settle
  • Single entry - Cannot leave and re-enter UK
  • No extension - Cannot extend this visa type
  • Must leave after marriage - Unless switching to spouse visa

👫 Relationship Requirements

  • Genuine relationship - Proof of authentic romantic relationship
  • Met in person - Must have met your partner physically
  • Free to marry - Both parties legally able to marry
  • Marriage intentions - Clear evidence of wedding plans

💰 Financial Requirements

  • Adequate funds - Support yourself during UK stay
  • No public funds - Cannot claim benefits or housing
  • Sponsor support - UK partner may provide financial support
  • Return travel costs - Funds for journey home

📋 Documentation Requirements

  • Valid passport - 6+ months validity remaining
  • Relationship evidence - Photos, correspondence, visits
  • Wedding arrangements - Venue bookings, registrar contact
  • Accommodation details - Where staying in UK
  • Financial evidence - Bank statements, employment proof

Marriage Visitor Visa vs Fiancé Visa - Key Differences

🤔 Which Visa Should You Choose?

Our Manchester wedding visa solicitors help you decide:

Marriage Visitor Visa:

  • ✅ Quick processing (3-4 weeks)
  • ✅ Lower cost (£95 application fee)
  • ❌ Must leave UK after marriage
  • ❌ Cannot switch to spouse visa

Fiancé Visa:

  • ✅ Can switch to spouse visa after marriage
  • ✅ Path to UK settlement
  • ❌ Higher cost (£1,538 application fee)
  • ❌ Longer processing (8-12 weeks)
