What is an Unmarried Partner Visa?

An Unmarried Partner Visa allows non-EEA partners to join their British or settled partner in the UK. Our Manchester cohabitation visa solicitors explain the key requirements:

  • Genuine relationship - Must be in a long-term committed relationship
  • 2+ years cohabitation - Living together like married couple
  • Financial requirement - £18,600 minimum income threshold
  • English language - A1 level speaking and listening
  • Accommodation - Adequate housing in UK
  • No immigration breaches - Clean immigration history

Unmarried Partner Visa Requirements - Manchester Solicitors

Our unmarried partner visa lawyers in Manchester help couples meet these essential requirements:

💕 Relationship Requirements

  • Genuine and subsisting relationship - Authentic romantic partnership
  • 2+ years cohabitation - Living together in relationship akin to marriage
  • Met in person - Must have physically met each other
  • Cannot marry - Not just choosing not to marry, but prevented from marrying
  • Intention to live together - Permanent life together in UK

💰 Financial Requirements

  • £18,600 minimum income - UK sponsor's annual earnings
  • Additional £3,800 - For first child
  • Additional £2,400 - For each subsequent child
  • Employment evidence - 6 months employment history
  • Savings alternative - £62,500+ in savings as alternative

🏠 Accommodation Requirements

  • Adequate accommodation - Not overcrowded housing
  • Sole occupation - Or sharing with family only
  • Housing documents - Tenancy agreement or mortgage proof
  • Property inspection - May be required by Home Office
